Frequently Asked Quetions

How is the IELTS coaching structured? How does it progress from day 1?
We follow the curriculum basis of the IELTS course you join us for. The first 4-6 classes are dedicated to IELTS structural understanding of all four modules, followed by conceptual learning, strategies & tips, then practice, and finally evaluation. Assignment evaluation is a daily feature of your IELTS coaching with us. The trainer also helps you add newer vocabulary, idioms, and phrases to your arsenal which optimises your writing and speaking ability.
Do you guarantee my success and the score I need?
We guarantee your improvement when you join us for IELTS classroom coaching or IELTS online coaching, and you have our assurance on the quality of IELTS training we impart; however, we cannot own your performance during your IELTS exam. We pledge to guide you in the correct way through our effective IELTS training methodology, which has the potential to significantly improve your performance. But we do not guarantee you any specific outcome. Someone who does must be a wizard.
What are the qualifications of the IELTS trainers you have? And what's the process of selection?
The first 2 things we weigh during the selection process are a positive attitude and a flair for knowledge sharing. IELTS certification and excellent communication skills then follow. Your IELTS trainer has vast experience of many success stories; you would be another feather in his/her cap.
How much time is needed to be IELTS ready? And how many hours should I dedicate each day to my preparation?
The IELTS preparation time is subject to your current level of the language and your availability for IELTS preparation. Students with an English language background typically require 1.5 to 2 months of preparation with around 1-2 hours of self-study and practice each day, beyond the IELTS coaching hours. Our IELTS trainers guide you in the correct way of IELTS preparation so that your practice yields better results.
